Project Insight

NASA's Insight Project opens a new window of exploration into the geological subsurface of mars. The lander is an improved upon design of the resilient NASA Mars Phoenix lander. The robotic arm built within the lander is over 5 feet in length and it lifts multiple different types of equipment such as a seismometer and heat flow probe from the deck and places them on the surface of the red planet.

Seen below is an discriptive photograph of the landers arm mechansism. This mechansism is responsible for the management of the scientific instruments installed on the lander itself.
